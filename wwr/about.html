River Rat Rafting Expeditions

Family fun adventures for every age! We offer family, or team, trust building excersizes, games, hikes, river stories, history lessons about the area, and much more. It is our mission to introduce participants to a fun, engaging, relationship building experience on the river. There's nothing better than a good old mud fight with friends and family members on the river.

History

Our story begins in 1981. Our family began white water rafting different sections of the Colorado River. We enjoyed our experiences so much that we decided to share them with others. We started out slow with small groups and eventually obtained enough gear, experienced river guides, and a great system to allow for group expeditions. The birth of River Rat Rafting Expeditions was then established in 1990.
