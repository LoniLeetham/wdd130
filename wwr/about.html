River Rat Rafting Expeditions

Family fun adventures for every age! It is our mission to introduce participants to a fun, engaging, relationship-building experience on the river. Strengthening families and building life-long friendships.

History

Our story begins in 1981. Our family began white water rafting different sections of the Colorado River. We enjoyed our experiences so much that we decided to share them with others. We started out slow with

small groups and eventually obtained enough gear, experienced river guides, and a great system to allow for group expeditions. The birth of River Rat Rafting Expeditions was then established in 1990.
