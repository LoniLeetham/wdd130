What to expect on your Rafting Expedition

Relaxing 4-day Yampa River Rafting Adventure with Safety in Mind

River Rat Rafting Expeditions is Utah's premier whitewater river rafting outfitter since 1981. We will be exploring the Yampa River. The Yampa river is a portion of the Colorado River. Your trip will include all of the river rafting gear that you'll need for a fun, safe, and successful adventure. We provide 3 meals as well as snack options. All you need to bring is a backpack filled with clothes and toiletries, a water bottle, sleeping bag, pillow, and a smile.

Family Friendly Fun

Perfect for ages 8 years old up to 80 and all skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned river rat or a newbie, you will fit right in.
