Overview

Purpose

River Rat Rafting Expeditions is Utah's premier whitewater river rafting outfitter since 1981. We will be exploring the Yampa River. The Yampa river is a portion of the Colorado River. Your trip will include all of the river rafting gear that you'll need for a fun, safe, and successful adventure. We provide 3 meals as well as snack options. All you need to bring is a backpack filled the items on your packing list, a sturdy water bottle, sleeping bag, pillow, and a smile.

Audience

Perfect for ages 8 years old up to 80 and all skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned river rat or a newbie, you will fit right in. Question: could my 3 children, ages 12, 15, and 17, join us on this trip? Answer: Yes, they will love all of the amazing experiences they will have during our expedition. Question: I'm not a great swimmer. Will I be okay on this trip? Answer: Yes! We make sure all of our clients are safe with appropriate safety gear like life jackets, helmets, and experienced boating guides. We also go over safety rules before our expeditions begin to ensure a safe trip for all levels of swimming abilities. Question: I don't have any life jackets for my children, do you have safety gear that will fit children? Answer: Yes, we do! We have a lot of different sized life jackets and helmets that will accomodate individuals of ALL sizes. From small children to large adults. We have you ALL correctly fitted before we launch on the river.
