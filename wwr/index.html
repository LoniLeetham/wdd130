River Rat Rafting Expeditions

All-inclusive Guided Rafting Adventures on the Yampa River

Our company takes safety seriously. We check our helmets, life jackets, paddles, and rafts before and after each expedition. Before we set out on the water, trainings and indepth instructions will be given to everyone.

We offer 3 expedition options for you to choose from. Each exepidition is unique and fun. You are able to decide how long you're able to enjoy the beauty of the river.

We pride ourselves as a successful and thriving guided rafting company. We hire only the best guides to help you enjoy your experience. We have been running the Yampa River since 1981. We are happy to share all of the fun details and history of the river and canyon.
